Real Estate & PropTech

What AI enablement looks like for real estate firms, brokerages, and property technology companies managing high-volume workflows.

🏘️

Property Research Agent

Aggregates data from MLS listings, public records, zoning databases, and market reports to produce structured property briefs in minutes. Analysts spend time analyzing deals, not compiling data — and coverage expands without adding headcount.

📄

Lease Abstraction Agent

Parses commercial lease agreements and extracts key terms — rent schedules, renewal options, exclusivity clauses, and critical dates — into a standardized format. Review cycles that took days now take hours, and nothing slips through the cracks.

💬

Tenant Communication Bot

Handles routine tenant inquiries about maintenance requests, lease questions, and building policies around the clock. Staff focus on complex issues while response times drop to minutes and tenant satisfaction improves across the portfolio.

📡

Deal Pipeline Monitor

Tracks comparable sales, cap rate movements, and market velocity across your target submarkets. Deal teams receive weekly intelligence briefs without manual research, staying ahead of market shifts and identifying opportunities before they hit the broader market.

🔎

Document Due Diligence Agent

Reviews stacks of due diligence documents — title reports, inspection findings, environmental assessments — and flags issues, inconsistencies, and missing items before closing. Reduces the risk of surprises and compresses the time between LOI and close.

📬

CRM & Outreach Automation

Scores and prioritizes your contact database, drafts personalized outreach sequences for brokers and investors, and updates CRM records based on communication activity. Your team stays focused on relationships while the agent handles the follow-up cadence.
